Chess DX

This is a game I recently started making. It mostly functions like a normal Chess game but with a couple modifications as shown in the youtube video

How it works:

You start with 15HP and the first person to have a HP of 0 or a lower HP at turn 50 loses

Checks deal 1DMG, Checkmates deal 3 DMG, the rat deals 5 DMG

If a piece is protected by another piece, your king is not able to take it
